Get Involved

As of Spring 2018, ACM meets in Dunn 206 every Friday, at 4pm. ACM-W meets as announced in ACM.

We hope to engage interested students through regular meetings, lightning talks, and interactive workshops.


Attend ACM

The best way you can get involved is to coming to regular Friday meetings. The meetings kick-off with some announcements, and then we go over our upcoming events.
We encourage everyone to share project updates and lightning talks.
Lightning talks are quick presentations on what's going on in tech, personal projects, anything at all.

Attend ACM-W

ACM-W times are announced at ACM.
ACM-W meetings are run by a selected moderator. The moderator picks a topic, presents on it, and opens discussion.
ACM-W is a safe space. We often talk about the department climate and diversity issues in STEM.
All are welcome.

Leadership

We are always looking for help. All volunteers are listed on the organization page.
We are looking for regular admins. Admins are invited to executive board meetings, and the level of involvement is entirely up to the volunteer. It's eboard-lite. Help your local community, have full autonomy over your workload, and add something to your resume.

Become a National ACM Chapter Member

We encourage all members to become a member of SUNY Potsdam's National ACM chapter. It only costs $19 and you can check out all the benefits here.
