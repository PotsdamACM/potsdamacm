About the SUNY Potsdam ACM-W

ACM-W meets on Sundays when scheduled, always announced at ACM.

A typical ACM-W meeting: a presentation on the set topic, then discussion.
Our club is structured differently than most. Each meeting is run entirely by one ACM-W organizer; this organizer serves as the speaker and the moderator. The position of the moderator cycles between the organizers, and any student can be an organizer.
The SUNY Potsdam ACM-W is completely driven by student volunteers.
This has brought out the best in our ACM-W members; no one does work they don't want to do, and moderators are free to chose the topics they are passionate about.

Our Mission

>   To support, celebrate, and advocate for the full engagement of women interested in technology and science on the SUNY Potsdam campus.
>   To promote solidarity among the women in STEM on our campus.
>   To create a safe space for the open discussion of sexism and discrimination in STEM.
>   To connect students with female mentors.
>   To organize the NYCWiC trip, with the goal of participating in the poster session.
