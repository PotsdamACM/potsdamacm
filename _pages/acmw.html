About the SUNY Potsdam ACM-W

ACM-W meets on Sundays when scheduled, always announced at ACM.

Our club is different.
Each meeting is run entirely by one ACM-W organizer. Typically, a topic is chosen. A quick presentation on the topic is given, and then the floor opens for discussion.
Club President cycles between the listed organizers every club meeting, and any student can be an organizer.

Our Mission

>   To support, celebrate, and advocate for the full engagement of women interested in technology and science on the SUNY Potsdam campus.
>   To promote solidarity among the women in STEM on our campus.
>   To create a safe space for the open discussion of sexism and discrimination in STEM.
>   To connect students with female mentors.
>   To organize the NYCWiC trip, with the goal of participating in the poster session.
