About the SUNY Potsdam ACM

ACM meets every Friday at 4pm in Dunn 206, except Fridays before academic breaks.

ACM-W meets on Sundays when scheduled, announced at ACM.


Our Mission

The SUNY Potsdam ACM and ACM-W work to:

>   Create a collaborative environment for students interested in technology and computing
>   Establish an inclusive, welcoming environment for all students interested in technology and computing
>   Provide support services for students taking computer science courses
>   Guide and assist students through the professional world of technology and computing

we are...

a national chapter

SUNY Potsdam's ACM is recognized by the national chapter.
SUNY Potsdam's ACM-w is recognized by the national chapter.

ACM is widely recognized as the premier membership organization for computing professionals, delivering resources that advance computing as a science and a profession; enable professional development; and promote policies and research that benefit society.

a local chapter and student organization

SUNY Potsdam's ACM is recognized by SUNY Potsdam's Student Government Association.
SUNY Potsdam's ACM-W is associated with SUNY Potsdam's Center For Diversity.

SUNY Potsdam's ACM is an active member of SGA. We attend Senate and committees, and we participate in collaborative events such as BearCon and Multicultural Weekend.
